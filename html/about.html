I have decided to change my live
by making 50 or even more web projects

One day I was thinking about myself and about my future career. What I can do to make it easier and have more time for me and my family?

In the past I was studying Telecommunication at University of Technology in Wroclaw. During my studies I tried to start learning programming few times, unfortunately always gave up. I was waiting to change it long time. That day has come. Few weeks ago I bought 3 books about HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. I started reading them one by one. In the meantime I was doing a lot of online courses and many tutorials. Of course you cannot start without basics so books are really useful, but to learn something, you need to practise. I started by creating website for my dad’s company. It was my first experience shock. I felt like a person who has never read anything about web development. Simply positioning was really hard, but every minute, every hour and every day it was better and better. Finally I finished my dad’s website after about one week.

After that I felt that I need to do something different. Something which will help me to learn more things. Something really helpful which show my skills and be my online CV. I was reading different blogs and than decided to make something similar, but also mine. I decided make 50 or even more different web projects. Every project will contain different topic, function and mechanism. After about 50 projects I hope I will know much more about web programing than before and will have enough knowledge to start some big professional projects.

We will see what future bring to me :)
